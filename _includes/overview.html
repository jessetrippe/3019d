Experience the best of Alki Beach living in this modern, light-filled townhome, built in 2020, featuring sleek finishes, an open layout, and energy-efficient design. The first floor welcomes you with an open-concept living area, showcasing floating stairs, premium appliances, and expansive windows that fill the space with natural light. A ductless mini-split ensures comfort while keeping energy costs low.

On the middle floor, two versatile bedrooms that share a full bath. The third floor is an oversized primary suite, offering plenty of space and privacy. The large, spa-like bathroom features dual sinks, ample storage, and a luxurious walk-in shower—but it’s not ensuite, allowing guests to access it without walking through your bedroom—perfect for entertaining on the rooftop deck.

Located just steps from West Seattle’s best dining and cafés, enjoy morning coffee at Ampersand Café, grab a casual bite at Cactus or Harry's, or indulge in waterfront seafood at Driftwood or Salty’s. Scenic beachside trails, kayaking, and vibrant nightlife are all within easy reach. With no HOA fees, energy-efficient heating & cooling, and off-street parking, this townhome delivers the ideal blend of modern comfort, coastal charm, and urban convenience.
